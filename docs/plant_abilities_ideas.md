## Plant ideas
### Balistic seed dispersal
1. **Squirting Cucumber** (*Ecballium elaterium*) - pod hangs downwards, when touched, the pod shoots downward and shoots a slimy fluid filled with seeds into the air
    - Attack: low (maybe it could be high if the pod dealt damage to the plant?)
    - Defense: low
    - Status: slows, but only close by
    - Dispersal: High (can maybe hit airborn enemies?)
    - Size:  
        - Width: up to 3'
        - Height: 6"- 12"
2. **Orange/Spotted Jewelweed** (*Impatiens capensis*) - seedpods are small, but explode outwards wuite violently
    - Attack: high
    - Defense: medium
    - Status: none
    - Dispersal: wide and fast
    - Size
        - Width:
        - Height: 1-5' high

# Abilities
1. Grafting  
    - combine two plants, but one has dominant features. the plant being grafted to (attched to) another plant is the dominant, but aspects of the plant receiving the graft flow through the graft
        - example: orange tree gives a bonus (utility), rose bush has thorns (attack)
            - if you graft orange tree to rosebush, you get oranges that have thorns (maybe gives a more potent affect, but damages the receiving plant)
            - if you graft a rose to an orange tree, you get an attacker that also gives a passive bonus
